Beacon Hunt

How to Play:

DON'T START YET. Read on...

The timer countdown will commence when you start the game. There is no way to stop the timer because you'll already have a peek at the clues. No cheating allowed.

After starting, you will have a list of beacon sites to find. Click on one to reveal a clue. Reveal additional clues up to three, but your score will take a hit.

You can go back and forth between beacons and clues and find them in any order. However, you must be on the respective hint page to register the beacon.

Start the hunt when all teams are assembled and ready.
